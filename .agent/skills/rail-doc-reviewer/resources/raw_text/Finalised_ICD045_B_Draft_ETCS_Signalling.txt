1. Introduction
1.1 Purpose
The purpose of this Interface Control Document (ICD) is to,
1. Document the QTMP and Sequence approach to manage, monitor and control scope with respect to
ICD45.
2. Document the QTMP DDR design packages to enable Sequence to review, develop and align ETCS
design.
3. Provide a framework that QTMP and Sequence will work within to review, align and potentially
modify QTMP and Sequence Design.
4. Document the current approach related to procurement, construction, test and commission of future
works.
5. Provide a framework that QTMP and Sequence Alliance will work within to review, align and
potentially modify planned activities related to procurement, construction, test and commission of
future works.
6. Document content that will satisfy QTMP ICD requirements noting Sequence may require additional
but separate supplementary documents.
1.2 Scope
ICD045 focuses on the ETCS signalling interfaces within the stabling yard between Downer/DTI and
Sequence Alliance, where Downer/DTI will be performing most of the civil works (designing and installing the
facility infrastructure) and Sequence complete the ETCS design, procure, test and commission.
